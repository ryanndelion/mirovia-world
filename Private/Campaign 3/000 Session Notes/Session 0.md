# General TTRPG Guidelines
### 1 - Play as a group (That includes the GM)
This covers a large portion of the out of game and in-game expectations
- Respect everyone in the game. Don't cancel last minute
- Communicate
	- Set expectations/boundaries early (Session 0 Ideally)
		- Safety
		- Ensure Consent
		- Communicate discomfort
	- Needs and wants
		- For Self
		- For Character
	- Conflict resolution
	- Ask questions
		- Clarify and confirm
	- Highlight fun stuff
- Prioritise what is fun for the group
	- Buy into other characters
	- Riff off people
	- Play together
### 2 - Buy into the game
It is the player’s responsibility to care
- Care about characters and the world
	- Know the setting
- Pay attention / take Notes
- Make characters that belong in the world
### 3 - Be ready with your character
This is the interface from which you play the game know it well
- Know your character
	- Who your character is
	- How they fit in the setting
- Mechanics
	- Know the rules
	- What your character can do
	- Be ready on your turn in combat
